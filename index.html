Use Steam to play all your old games

Just because old consoles aren't sold anymore doesn't mean you can't enjoy their games.

Setup is easy

Just download Ice from the link above and run it. That will put a ROMs folder in your home directory.

Make sure Steam is closed, put your ROMs in the correct folder, and run Ice again. Open Steam again, and you are done!

Preconfigured for 360 controllers

All emulators are preconfigured to work well with an Xbox 360 controller. To exit an emulator, hit the ESC key, or use the included JoyToKey setup and press SELECT then START on your controller.
